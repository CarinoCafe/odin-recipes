Pesto Pizza

A pizza recipe by Shelly Williams. In this recipe we will learn how to make a tasty pesto pizza in about 20 minutes!

This recipe yeilds about eight servings of pizza. Besure to have all of the ingredients on hand, listed below.

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • 1/2 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
  • 1/2 small red onion, chopped
  • 1 (4 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
